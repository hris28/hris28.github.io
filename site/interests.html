Dance

I've trained in Bharatanatyam for over a decade at Nrityam Dance Academy and won Best Choreography at Festival of India. I dabble in hip hop, Bollywood fusion, piano, vocals, and songwriting. If I'm not at a computer, I'm usually moving to something.

Other loves

Chess shaped how I think about patterns. I've ranked in the top 34 female players in my age group through USCF and still play casually. I compete in Shakespeare monologue and sonnet competitions, and was cast as Marci in my high school production of Almost, Maine.

Offline, you can find me cooking something new, choreographing, losing at Catan, or over-engineering a Notion template.
